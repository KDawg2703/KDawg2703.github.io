About

The analyst behind the work

I'm a cybersecurity professional developing expertise across SOC operations and GRC frameworks. My approach combines hands-on technical investigation with a governance mindset — I don't just detect threats, I help organisations understand what they mean for business risk.

My SOC work focuses on building detection logic grounded in real attacker behaviour (MITRE ATT&CK), running home lab investigations, and producing incident reports that communicate findings clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences.

On the GRC side, I work with NIST CSF and ISO 27001 to build risk registers, gap assessments, and security policies that are actually usable — not just compliance checkbox exercises.
